Greenland and Iceland, sigh
Hi, first let me say that I really like this game as IMO it offers something different, and is still packed with plenty of challenges and stuff for the player to find out.

One problem I've consistently encountered on nearly every campaign is transmission to Greenland and Iceland.

I realise one is supposed to have a strategy when approaching this game, but the idea is also to adapt. Throughout the 50+ games I've played, those islands have felt very "binary" to me - either I lucked out and gott them, or I didn't. Even with a disease that has Lvl. 2 in Cold & Water transmission perks, I can't consistently get them and I end up just giving up and starting in Russia/Canada every time (then mandatorily build around Cold & Water). It seems to go against the general idea behind the game :(

As far as I know, there are only rare, disease-specific ways of infecting islands with closed harbors, like Trojan Planes and Spore Drops. (I heard Extreme Bioaerosol does it, but no luck for me). It's just not very clear for the player... moreover, it makes for those scenarios where you're just staring at the screen, half-unsure if your playthrough is doomed or not.

So, I'd like to suggest 2 things:
  • Slightly increased boat traffic to Greenland, and perphaps an additional country property to account for different disease evolution preferences..
  • A "Retry Scenario" button at the escape menu. I rather often get the urge to restart a scenario that isn't going well, so I can perfect my approach. I'd like to be able to do that without going through the Main Menu > Disease Selection > Gene selection everytime.

That is just my humble feedback as a new player, I expect not everyone will agree. Thanks!
